# 🃏 Three-Card-Myrtle

**Three Card Myrtle** is a fast-paced poker card game where players compete against the dealer using just three cards. Make strategic betting decisions, choose whether to play or fold, and aim to build the highest-ranking poker hand to win each round.

## 🚀 Why Three Card Myrtle?

Three Card Myrtle combines the excitement of traditional poker with shorter, action-packed rounds. The simple rules make it easy to learn, while strategic betting and play-or-fold decisions keep every game engaging and rewarding.

## 📖 Game Rules

1. Place your initial bet.
2. Receive three cards.
3. Decide to **Play** or **Fold**.
4. The dealer reveals their hand.
5. The highest-ranking poker hand wins the round.
